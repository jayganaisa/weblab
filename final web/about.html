Nurali, Islambek and Bekzat are three friends, who always shared a passion for travel and aviation. Their desire to improve the experience of traveling and make the process of buying flight tickets more affordable and convenient led to the creation of Aitulines. Aitulines was founded in Astana, the capital of Kazakhstan, and in the shortest period of time became one of the leading players in the air transportation industry. We are firmly convinced that affordable tickets is not a myth. Founders trio are experienced entrepreneurs and had extensive background in the field of information technology and travel management. They organized a team of talented developers and marketing experts to create an innovative and functional online platform for purchasing flight tickets.

Aitulines was developing with an immense speed. A team responsible for the projext always try their best in order to keep the web-site up to date and optimize it's usage for the people. In addition, CEOs' established partnerships with large airlines around the world to provide their customers an access to a large number of flight destinations. Aitulines also showed social responsibility by participating in the environmental protection programs and reducing greenhouse gas emissions campaigns. The company actively supported the initiatives in sustainable tourism and environmental consciousness. Its founders, Nurali, Islambek and Bekzat, are proud of their achievement and see their future in the development of even more advanced technologies and providing customers with the best travel experience.
